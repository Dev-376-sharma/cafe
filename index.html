Common Cyber Threats

Stay informed about the most prevalent cybercrime methods targeting citizens today.

UPI Fraud

Fraudsters trick users into sharing payment credentials or approving fake UPI transaction requests through social engineering.

Phishing Attacks

Fake emails, SMS, or links designed to look legitimate, used to steal sensitive information like passwords and card details.

OTP Scams

Attackers manipulate victims—often posing as bank officials—into sharing one-time passwords to hijack accounts.

Deepfake Calls

AI-generated voice or video impersonating relatives or officials, used to demand money or sensitive information.
